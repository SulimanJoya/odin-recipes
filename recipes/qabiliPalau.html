Qabili Palau

The national dish of Afghanistan is Qabili Palau (also spelled Kabuli Palau or Palao or Palaw or Pilau or Pilaf).
Why not make it on August 19 when Afghanistan celebrates their independence day.

Ingredients

  • Oil: Any neutral cooking oil. I have used sunflower oil.
  • Almonds: You may use store-bought sliced almonds.
  • Veggies: carrots and onions
  • Raisins: I have used golden raisins in these images as I ran out of black ones.
  • Sugar
  • Meat: Use mutton or lamb with bone. You may make this with chicken too.
  • Ground spices: cumin powder, cardamom powder, and black pepper powder
  • Basmati rice
  • Whole spices: green cardamom, cloves and cinnamon stick

Steps

  1. Fried the almonds and removed and kept it aside.
  2. Fried the carrots and then the raisins and again removed and kept them aside.
  3. Browned the onions and meat along with spices.
  4. Pressure-cooked the meat with water.
  5. Removed the meat and kept it aside.
